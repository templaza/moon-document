# Introduction

Moon Framework was developed with simplicity and ease of use in mind. Moon lets you manage every aspect of your Moodle website without writing a single line of code. Its intuitive interface helps you build performant and responsive websites the easy way.

Moon is also a powerful modern framework for designers & advanced developers alike. Designers can appreciate its incredible flexibility to build exactly what is in their minds without compromises. For developers, it ensures extremely fast and robust Moodle-based websites based on the latest web technologies standard.


:::tip[Voila]
Today Moon is possibly the most powerful companion to support Moodle themes!
:::

## You are welcome to participate.
Moon is an Open Source project, and you are welcome to participate in its development. You can help by reporting bugs, suggesting features, or even contributing code. The Moon community is active and welcoming, making it easy for newcomers to get involved.
